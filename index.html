Tualatin Valley Youth Lacrosse League

TVYLL provides all youth (girls and boys), grades K through 8, living within the Beaverton School District boundary, an opportunity to participate in a competitive lacrosse program emphasizing the fundamentals of the game, good sportsmanship, and the spirit of competition

TVYLL is comprised of six Beaverton youth lacrosse associations and is an affiliate of the Tualatin Hills Park and Recreation District (THPRD)
